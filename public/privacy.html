SWEATSQUAD

PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: June 7, 2026

SweatSquad is a fitness challenge web application that helps friend groups create and track workout challenges together. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you use SweatSquad, we collect the following:

  • Account information — your display name, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account
  • Google account info — if you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. We do not receive your Google password
  • Workout data — challenges you create or join, and your logged workout metrics
  • Chat messages — messages you send in the group chat, including @mentions
  • Device info — your browser timezone and a notification token if you enable push notifications

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and operate the SweatSquad app
  • To display your progress on challenge leaderboards
  • To send push notifications (only if you opt in)
  • To calculate badges and points based on your activity
  • To remember your preferences across sessions

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored using Google Firebase. You can review Google's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. DATA SHARING

SweatSquad is a social app — some information is visible to other users by design:

  • Your display name appears on leaderboards, in chat, and next to your workout logs
  • Your workout progress is visible to others in your group
  • Chat messages are visible to all app users

Your email address and password are never visible to other users.

5. PUSH NOTIFICATIONS

If you enable push notifications, we store a device token to send you notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your browser or device settings.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

To request account deletion or access to your data, contact us at the email below.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

SweatSquad is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of SweatSquad after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
